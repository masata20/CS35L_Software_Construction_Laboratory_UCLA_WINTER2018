1. Upper left pane
   Series of commit to the repository, in the form of tree.

2. Upper middle pane
   Shows the person's name and email who commit which is in the same row in the (1).

3. Upper right pane
   Shows the date of commit which is the same row in the (1).

4. Lower left pane
   Shows the Diff between old version and new version.
   We can also see either old version or new version only by switching the
   diagonal check box.

5. Lower right pane
    Shows list of files impacted by the selected commit
